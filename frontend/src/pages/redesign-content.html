DigitalLearn has a new look

You're in the right place. We've rebuilt DigitalLearn.org to be easier to use on any device, with the same free courses you count on.

Already in the middle of a course? Your lessons and progress are still here, right where you left them.

Working with a digital navigator or librarian? They can help you find your way around the new design.

Why we made changes

Built from what you told us

Over the past two years we listened to learners, librarians, and trainers across the country. They told us the site needed to work better on phones, make courses easier to find, and be readable for every age. This redesign is our answer.

What's new for you
